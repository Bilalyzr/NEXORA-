The SIMS Project

A centralized industrial governance proposal by Startup NEXORA.

NEXORA proposes Thozhirporul, the SIPCOT Smart Industrial Monitoring System (SIMS). This centralized platform enables seamless data collection, real-time tracking, and advanced analytics. The system allows industries to submit operational data digitally while providing authorities with a unified dashboard for monitoring, compliance management, and reporting.

The Challenge

Fragmented Data

Industries rely on manual spreadsheets and emails, leading to data silos and reporting delays.

Manual Compliance

Compliance tracking is paper-based, making it difficult to verify and audit in real-time.

Lack of Transparency

Government officials struggle with centralized monitoring and data-driven decision-making.

The NEXORA's Solution

NEXORA provides a unified intelligent platform designed specifically for SIPCOT industrial ecosystems.

  • Centralized Monitoring Platform
  • AI-Powered Governance
  • Real-Time Analytics Dashboards
  • Automated Compliance Tracking
